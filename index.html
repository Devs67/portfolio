01 — About

From the workshop floor
to the classroom.

I started in mechanical engineering — drafting parts in SolidWorks, fabricating frames, designing moulds. Somewhere between a solar-powered farm robot and a wave-energy rig, I realised I liked teaching the build as much as doing it.

Today I lead STEM program delivery for Creya Learning & Research across more than ten schools, and I'm building Experion Robotics Academy, my own venture for lab setup, curriculum and trainer deployment in partner schools — taking the same hands-on, Design-Thinking-first approach I used as a student into the classrooms I now run.

I care about programs that are real, repeatable and measured — not one-off demos. Every curriculum I build has a project a student can actually hold at the end of it.
